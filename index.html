About Me

I'm a DevOps Engineer currently working at Hashedin by Deloitte in Hyderabad. With over 6 years of IT experience and 5+ years specializing in Cloud & DevOps, I bring deep expertise in building, automating, and securing modern infrastructure.

My journey started as a System Administrator, where I built a solid foundation in infrastructure management. I gradually transitioned into Cloud Engineering with AWS and GCP, and eventually into the DevOps space — where I found my passion for CI/CD pipelines, container orchestration, Infrastructure as Code, and security-first practices.

I'm passionate about bridging the gap between development and operations, enabling teams to ship faster and more reliably.
